Creig 24 kids' mountain bike / age 8+

The Creig 24 is a lightweight hard-tail mountain bike for junior riders who want a bike that can handle anything, from local trails to big days in the mountains.

View the Creig size guide

Orders for European delivery

Creig specification
  • Confident, responsive handling make the Creig 24 your perfect trail thrill-seeker
  • Rock Shox 30 gold air suspension fork with 60mm of travel for smooth and tuneable performance
  • Islabikes proportionally sized, 30mm direct mount cranks featuring optimised 141.5mm narrow Q-factor
  • Ready to go tubeless with Stan's Crest tubeless rims and Vee tire Crown Gems
  • Designed with 24 inch wheels for riders with a 58cm inside leg upwards.

Progressive frame geometry

The Creig 24’s long, low and relaxed geometry, combined with a short stem and wide handlebar, equips you to tackle anything the trail throws at you.

From XC to DH, it’s proven that bigger wheels roll over rough terrain better, plus they increase traction and stability. Islabikes are designed to accommodate the largest diameter wheels possible without compromising the perfect riding position. Why is this important? Bigger wheels “smooth out” the ride and provide more grip due to greater tyre contact with the ground.

RockShox 30 Gold Air Fork

Due to their relative light weight, smaller riders often don’t experience the benefits of a suspension fork as they aren’t heavy enough to make the fork physically move. With air-sprung suspension we can adjust the fork’s characteristics to suit the lighter rider, meaning you can get in on the trail-calming benefits of suspension.

Exclusive super narrow Q-factor cranks

We’ve always gone the extra mile when it comes to getting the fit of our bikes “just right”. Our proportionally sized cranks are 140mm in length to ensure you are not over-extending with each pedal stroke. The Q-factor (the horizontal distance between the pedals) is ultra-narrow at just 141.5mm to further optimize your pedalling effort. Finally, they feature direct-mount technology to enable easy change of narrow-wide chainrings from 26t to 32t.

Lightweight tubeless-ready wheelset

The Creig comes trail ready with super lightweight Stan’s Crest tubeless ready rims and Vee Tire Crown Gem tyres, featuring a fast rolling, super grippy tread pattern.

The Creig is no slouch on the climbs

We’ve spec’d the Creig 24 with a 1x chainset to further reduce weight. The combination of a 26t front narrow/wide chainring and 11 – 40t wide ratio cassette ensure the spread of gears needed to tackle the steepest terrain.

Carefully prepared by us

Islabikes trained mechanics will assemble and adjust your bike prior to dispatch whilst also fitting any accessories. When it arrives with you, following some minor assembly on arrival, your bike will be ready to go. Your bike comes with a set of hex keys for setup on arrival.
