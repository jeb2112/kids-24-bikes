Flowdown - Youth Mountain Bike (24") - Orange

The Diamondback Flowdown 24" is an excellent choice for the young mountain biker. The lightweight hardtail design, disc brakes, quality Shimano components and 7 speed drivetrain provides the aspiring rider with a versatile bike that is great for all around riding on a variety of trails, and the Shimano mega-range freewheel will help them get up any hills they encounter on their journey.

Specifications

  • Frame Specs Frame: 6061 T6 heat treated aluminum frame
  • Rear Shock: N/A
  • Fork: Vaxa 60mm travel w/ pre-load adjust
  • Cable Routing: External guided w/ full length housing
  • Rear Derailleur: Shimano Altus M310, 7 speed
  • Front Derailleur: N/A
  • Shifters: Shimano Rapid Fire M315 (RH), 7 speed
  • Cog Set: Shimano TZ500 Hyperglide, Mega Range 14-34T, 7 speed, freewheel
  • Chain: KMC Z7
  • Crankset: ProWheel Alloy, 9/16*152mm, w/ bash ring
  • Bottom Bracket: Gineyea BB73-68, square taper, sealed cartridge
  • Front Brake: JAK 8 Mechanical Disc, 160mm rotor
  • Rear Brake: JAK 8 Mechanical Disc, 160mm rotor
  • Brake Levers: JAK Alloy
  • Headset: 1 1/8" Threadless
  • Seat post: CAVU Alloy 27.2mm x 250mm
  • Handlebar: CAVU, 31.8mm*680mm
  • Stem: CAVU 50mm, 4 Bolt
  • Saddle: CAVU MTB
  • Pedals: 9/16" Resin body, flats
  • Grips: CAVU Lock-on w/ closed end
  • Rims: CAVU Aluminum double wall, 32 hole, 24"
  • Front Tire: 24" x 2.3" for all-terrain
  • Rear Tire: 24" x 2.3" for all-terrain
  • Tubeless Ready: No
  • Front Hub: CAVU, 36 H alloy w/ quick release
  • Rear Hub: CAVU, 36 H, alloy, w/ quick release

Features

  • Lightweight 6061 T6 aluminum frame with Diamondback's formed tubing features optimised wall thickness for a balance of strength, weight, and stiffness that translates to a responsive, and reliable ride
  • Vaxa 60mm travel front suspension fork with adjustable preload soaks up the bumps and offers smooth and confident control over various terrain
  • 24" x 2.3" aggressive MTB tires for traction that will bite into any trail conditions
  • JAK-8 mechanical disc brakes make for sure stopping power in both wet and dry conditions
  • Shimano Rapid Fire and Altus 7 speed drivetrain offers precise and deliberate shifting so you can tackle new terrain with confidence
  • Easy dependable resin flat pedals so you can jump on your new Diamondback back and ride with confidence
  • CAVU Comfort-Mountain saddle is equipped with a quick release seat post clamp so it's always easy to find the perfect saddle height.
  • CAVU Lock on grips for a confident and reliable connection point in wet or dry conditions
