BENEFITS

Easy to use

Aluminum frame, 8 speeds, trigger shifters, front/rear wheel quick release

Bike safety

Disc brakes, knobbly tires, derailleur guard, and Aheadset stem.

Cycling comfort

Suspension fork

TECHNICAL INFORMATIONS

Frame

Lightweight aluminum frame.
The Aheadset stem makes maintenance easy and makes the frame sturdier for riding on any terrain.
The rear S-bend stays optimize braking performance, making it easier to get through mud.

Suspension

Suspension fork dampens vibrations from rough and rugged paths and terrain.

Drivetrain

Change gears (8 speeds) using handlebar trigger shifters.

Brakes

Mechanical front and rear disc brakes for powerful, precise braking. Braking power is not affected by mud or bumps.

Handlebar/Stem/Steering

Suspension fork dampens vibrations on rough terrain for added comfort and better control.
The Aheadset stem provides additional safety for all-terrain cycling.

Wheels/Tires

Stronger, double-wall rims give the bike a resolutely sporty look.

Warranty

Lifetime warranty on manufacturing defects for all metal frames, rigid forks, stems, and handlebars Five-year warranty on all carbon frames and forks Two-year warranty on all other parts Excludes damages arising from normal wear and tear, misuse, negligence, and unauthorized modifications

Compatible accessories

100 Bike Stand Kid (Model Code 2694189)
20" and 24" Training Wheels Kid (Model Code 8550741)

After-Sales Service

Your bike's components (chain, derailleur, brakes, etc.) will need adjustments over time. Take advantage of a free tune-up for one year after purchase. Our technicians will carry out a 13-point check of your bike. Just visit your nearest Decathlon workshop with your Decathlon membership card and/or receipt.
