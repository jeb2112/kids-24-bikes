With its lightweight alloy frame, 24" wheels and smooth suspension fork, the Trail 24 built for smooth speed and adventure, on or off-road.

Where it thrives

On trails and through town

What it's built for

Comfort, Handling

Highlights

  • Light, durable SmartForm C3 Alloy frame and bump-smoothing front suspension fork
  • Wide range 8-speed gearing with light-action trigger shifter
  • 24" alloy rims with fast-rolling knobby tires

Sized Right

24" wheels offer the a great blend of speed, handling, and proper fit for riders between 4' and 4'9" (122cm - 145cm).

Light and Easy
Lightweight aluminum frame and components make the bike easy to pedal and maneuver, while the low standover make mounts and dismounts easier.
Up for Anything
With versatile on-road and off-road tires and a bump-smoothing suspension fork, Trail 24 is built to chase adventure, wherever the trail may lead.

Easy Shifting

Light action trigger shifters and wide-range 8-speed gearing make it easy to select the right gear for the terrain.
