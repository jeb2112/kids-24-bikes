Description

The Yama Jama 24" is a sweet, shredworthy 24” hardtail trail bike for the hard-charging 7-10-year-old.

It all starts with a 6061-T6 alloy frame using hydroformed and double-butted tubing, a semi-integrated head tube, a unique forged one-piece BB shell/yoke for added stiffness, ISCG-05 tabs and even an internal routing option for dropper posts. The Rock Shox Reba 26" fork with 100mm of travel - a huge performance upgrade that will also make service a breeze through the extensive support network for SRAM/Rock Shox products.

The drivetrain starts with the Brood Bike Co. Styx 145mm crank using a SRAM type direct-mount interface for the chainwheel and a sealed external bottom bracket. We add a Brood 32t narrow-wide alloy chainwheel and a bump up from 10 speeds to 11!  The shifting is handled with a SRAM NX derailleur and shifter combo to get things moving to the 11-42T 11 speed SRAM cassette. Stopping is handled by powerful Tektro AURIGA hydraulic discs. Like its smaller sibling, the 24” Yama Jama uses Brood tubeless-ready rims with a wide profile and tops it off with the amazing Brood Maxtion foldable, tubeless-ready tire. Touchpoints matter for kids too, so we’ve topped this Yama Jama off with Brood Tenderiderz lock-ons and the Brood Throne seat utilizing the patented Pivotal mechanism to get seat height down.

Approximate weight is 24bs (not incl. pedals).
