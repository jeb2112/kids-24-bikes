woom OFF AIR 6

Follow the call of the trails

The woom OFF AIR 6 offers children aged ten years and older a superlight and durable mountain bike which they can use to rock every trail. Tough terrain is no longer an intimidating obstacle but instead a fun challenge to be overcome. The special chainring ensures that the chain stays in place no matter how rough things get. A love of cycling becomes a passion for mountain biking.

Size: 26″ wheels
Weight: 24.3 lbs (without pedals)
Age: 10 - 14 years
Height: 55″ – 65″ height
Available editions:
OFF AIR / OFF
Follow the call of the trails

  • Lightweight Aluminum Frame

    The superlight aluminum frame with butted hydroformed tubes has a sporty geometry, long wheelbase and flat steering angle. The kink in the top tube makes it easy to dismount quickly in any situation.

  • Suspension Fork

    Comfort and control on the bumpiest terrain is a given thanks to the high-quality suspension fork, which can be adjusted to the rider’s weight. Tune the feel of the bike to suit the terrain with adjustable compression and rebound damping, plus lock-out the suspension when needed. These features ensure a good time, whether your child is shredding up the trails or cruising home.

  • Innovative Braking System

    With their excellent modulation combined with special brake levers designed for children's hands, the disc brakes increase safety and control in all situations, including on rough terrain and in wet conditions.

  • woom Stem

    Made from forged aluminum, the specially developed stem guarantees maximum stiffness with minimal weight. Thanks to its flip-flop design it can be turned over and mounted upside down to adjust the handlebar height.

  • Ergonomic Contact Points

    Comfort is very important to the riding experience and that's why the contact points on our bike are made specifically for kids. Our saddle is designed to accommodate the anatomy and the biomechanics of a young child. Our grips are ergonomically designed for kid’s hands. The pedals are durable, reflective, and slip-resistant.

  • Gears

    The precise and easy-to-operate gear system allows smooth shifting. The SRAM X5 system has nine gears suitable for a wide range of gradients thanks to 28 teeth in the front and an 11-36 cassette in the back.

  • High-Quality Tires

    The high-quality Schwalbe mountain bike tires with ADDIX SPEED rubber compound offer an ideal combination of low rolling resistance, maximum grip, good shock absorption and excellent durability.

  • Trigger Shifter

    The easy-to-use SRAM trigger shifter allows riders to shift smoothly and reliably through the nine gears using thumb and index finger.

  • Sticker set

    Personalize your bike! Every woom OFF is delivered complete with a set of stickers in four colors. Place them on your rims and top tube to personalize your bike!

Specifications

Frame
  • Frame material: lightweight, high-quality 6061 T6 aluminum with butted and hydroformed tubes

Gears

Wheels

  • 28 G14 spokes, double-crossed spoking
  • Aluminum nipples
Fork
  • Hydraulic air suspension fork with 90 mm travel, adjustable compression and rebound damping, plus lock-out
  • 1 1/8″ – 1″ baby taper shaft

  • Post mount for disc brake
Tires
