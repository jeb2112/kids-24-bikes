Geared-Up and Ready to Shred

The perfect mountain bike for kids loving life outdoors – blending the right-size frame, the right spec, and more attitude.

Where it thrives

Leisurely rides, Challenging trails

What it's built for

Trail riding fun, Handling, Agility

Highlights

  • Light, durable SmartForm C3 Alloy frame and bump-smoothing front suspension fork
  • Powerful disc brakes and light-action 8-speed shifting
  • 26" alloy rims with fast-rolling knobby tires

Crank up the fun

Trail 26 is built for progressing riders ages 9 to 13 between 142 cm and 153 cm - too big for kids’ bikes, but not quite big enough for adult bikes. Take it to the next level on the bike path or grow new skills on the dirt.

Big wheels, big fun

Full-sized 26-inch wheels eat bumps for breakfast and roll over roots with ease. They are more maneuverable than the larger 27.5-inch wheels found on many adult bikes and keep stand-over height low for stability and plenty of room to grow.

Sized just right
We crafted the Trail 26 with growing groms in mind, so the sizing and geometry are perfectly tuned for big kids who are ready for the next step towards adult bikes.

Ready to rip

The Trail 26 frame is lightweight for rocketing up hills and durable for tackling the rough stuff, plus it uses powerful disc brakes for quick stopping even on the steepest hills. Built to boost the fun, wherever you ride.
